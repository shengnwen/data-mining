Louise Riotte passed away in 1998 at the age of 89. 

Since her passing, the subject matter contained in this title is widely disseminated free on the internet.

Her father taught her to believe in and practice astrology, her mother was a practicing herbalist. 

Together they influenced her life and her books, Astrological Gardening, Sleeping with a Sunflower, and her Raising Animals by the Moon.
