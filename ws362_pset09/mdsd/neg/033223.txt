Too much psychobabble with little practical help that hasn't been offered a thousand times before.  The book should focus more on the opportunities the giftedness creates for the child both in education and in living a richer life.  Developing the &quot;hungry mind&quot; is key, and not letting the child's curiosity be stifled by a lowest-common-denominator public schooling system.  A much better book was &quot;Bringing Out the Best:  A Resource Guide for Parents of Young Gifted Children.&quot
