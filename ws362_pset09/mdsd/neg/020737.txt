Whenever you hear the name Hogan, you certainly are expecting greatness as Mr. Hogan himself set the standards higher than anyone else in the world of golf.  This book falls far short of Mr. Hogan's standard.  I have been in  the golf industry for over 20 years and around instruction for 15 of those,  and for beginner or expect alike one would be better off looking for a book  that is as easy to use and follow, as Hogan's classic and still standard,  Five Lessons
