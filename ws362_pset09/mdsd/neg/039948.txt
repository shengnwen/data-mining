Annoying.

I lost a lot of respect for Anne Rice with the reading of this book.  It made about as much sense, and meant as much to me as did Inga Muscio's non-fiction drivel.

I love sex - and, I am just a little kinky, too - but this?  This is pulp, this is an attempt to justify cheap porn spewed at the level of utter inhumanity; and considering that said humanity is never established, it is utterly worthless
