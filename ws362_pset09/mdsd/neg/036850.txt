MacDonald is an elitist: she wants what worked for her in her life to be the model for society and everybody else should just deal. But, as Richard Brookhiser wrote in his biography of Alexander Hamilton, what made Hamilton a great American is that he found a way to generalize his successes and achievements so that all Americans could follow in his footsteps. He didn't simply say: "I have great gifts, too bad for everybody else." That is the attitude that the Founders had for our Democracy. MacDonald, an elitist, would have sided with the King of England
