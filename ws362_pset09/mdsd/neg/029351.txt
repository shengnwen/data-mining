Although the pacing of the story is excellent, and the descriptions of the court of Ancient Egypt easily places you there as well, I still couldn't  get into the story. I put the blame on the characters inhabiting the novel.  Not one was worth worrying over, and if you don't care about a character,  you don't care what's happening to the character. All were unsympathtic, if  not downright abbrasive or cold. Also, I guess I've got too much Puritanism  in me, but all the incest going on put me off as well.
