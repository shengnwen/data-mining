The text is interesting at best.  The other edition "New Expansive Poetry" edited by R. S. Gwynn is a little less one-sided.  There are many other collections of essays that provide better introductions to New Formalist, New Narrative, and Expansive Poetry, namely Annie Finch's "After New Formalism," which is a much more open-minded texts that admits it own faults and is aware of the social-political aspects of form.
