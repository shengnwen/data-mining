Listening to the audio tape edition is a trying experience. Dr. Arrien has a soporific monotonous presentation througout much of the reading, which is punctuated by irregular breaths not synchronous with the phrasing. This is very distracting. There are a few kernels of brilliance, but you have to listen to a lot of chaff to discover them. Much of the story-telling is painstakingly drawn out, taking forever to get to the point. I kept hoping it would get better, but it never did. We would not have purchased it, had we had this information
