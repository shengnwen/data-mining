The first clue to the value of this book, the reader notices, is the scarcity of content -- the wide margins, the large print, and the scant pages.  The writer's timidity stems from the paucity of her message.  Someday Ms. Goldberg will look back on this book and ask, "Why didn't somebody stop me?"  Surely it represents a period of spiritual and psychological crisis.  Surely we will see again the bright, talented seeker after Truth that we loved in "quiet highway."  Surely, roshi's message and his dedication to the dharma live on. Yes, he was flesh and blood as are we all
