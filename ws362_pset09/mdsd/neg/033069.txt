Let's face it, I have a million things I'd rather spend my precious time doing that reading a book about a cute and lovable newlywed who has gotten into a horrible accident and suffered amnesia... only to remember halfway through the book that her darling husband is dead.

How rotten is that? Even a dose of "Ghost-esque" contact with the dearly departed loved one who assures her she will love again was not enough to restore my faith in humanity and Marian Keyes. As the English would say, "What is she playing at?
