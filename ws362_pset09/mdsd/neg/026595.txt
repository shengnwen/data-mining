I was hoping to read an informative, yet entertaining study on the history and impact of Hillary Clinton, whom I'm no fan of.  But the author constantly digresses into a pendantic rage. And ruminations follow one after another on Hillary's powers and why they should be stopped.  Yet she fails to make a case for why one should support or not support her views on Clinton.  The final 200+ pages take the reader through a thoroughly boring, month by month overview of Hillary's duties as First Lady.  By the final chapters I could barely keep awake
