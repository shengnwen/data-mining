I enjoyed Mr. Winegardner's first sequal very much.  Enough to look forward to this installment with anticipation.  I am nearly 400 pages in this nearly 500 page tome...and I still have only a vague idea why it was written.
The title of this review reflects my opinion that, like the Seinfeld show, this is a "book about nothing".  It is not funny, it is not suspenseful, it is not gory or violent, it is not interesting.  Yet I will finish it, just to say I did
