By 1945, the german army was bled white, gutted, undersupplied, out-numbered and lacking experienced officers and men.  The western front was manned by a desperate mixture of few veterans, old men, young boys and whatever the germans could get together.

The odds were NEVER even after 1943.  The Western front was a side show compared to the titanic battles that took place in the east.  The russians bore the brunt of the best units the german army could throw together
