Timeline is equivalent to a 99 cent taco. It's an indulgent bit of junk food.
Timeline starts with a Hillerman-esque scientist turning up in the middle of New Mexico - an intriguing idea, but goes to the land of mediocracy right after that filled with cartoon characters.
There are so many preposterous story elements that it's just really difficult to take seriously. The story is ever-predictable and ludicrous.
But, a 99 cent taco is worth the effort if you're real hungry.
Go back and read Andromeda Strain.
