At this juncture, I've only had opportunity to read the publisher's summary and a reader review, wherein the author's concentration on the U.S. data is majored.  But, &quot;the sixties&quot; were not a uniquely American  phenomenon.  Rather, virtually the whole world convulsed in the sixties  (see, e.g., South America; Europe) and these countries were free from such  influences as Viet Nam and the American style of child-rearing.  Thus, the  true root (and an informative understanding) of &quot;the sixties&quot;  must be sought beyond -- as well as within -- our borders
