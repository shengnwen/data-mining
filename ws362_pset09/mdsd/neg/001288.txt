A fascinating and socially important case, but yikes, this is a dry and wordy book! Amazingly, the author never tells us what happened to the &quot;heroine&quot; after the trial--did she die? When? How? Is she still alive, and some 160 years old? Get it out of the library and skim it if you're interested in French political history, but this is not a book you can buy to read over and over
