I spent 13 years as a Marine and at the time that the Crucible started I was an instructor at the School of Infantry. Civilians need to stay out of the Military way of life. If they want to take part in our life than Enlist or get commisioned. I am not saying that I dont like this book, it may be a great book but the subject is not good. Semper F
