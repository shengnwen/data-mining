It's a pretty good book for people who'd like to learn more about next generation wireless technologies. This 3G Wireless Demystified book covers 1G, 2G, 2.5G and 3G wireless networks and various air interfaces used and/or are proposed for these networks. It also does a very good job of listing applications and business opportunities for new wireless services.  While it doesn't provide in-depth design engineering level coverage of any particular wireless topic, it does offer a very good broad overview on everything pertaining to 3rd generation wireless
