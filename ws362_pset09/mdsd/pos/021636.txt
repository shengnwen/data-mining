An intelligent account of the absorption of the southwest into the Union as seen through the eyes of Navajos, Americans, Texans, Mexicans, New Mexicans, Confederates, Utes, Californians, Apaches, and Pueblos from the accounts of Kit Carson, Singing Grass, Narbona, John C. Fremont, Senator Thomas Hart Benton, President James Polk, Barboncito, Manuelito, General Stephen Watts Kearney, Susan Magoffin, Manuel Chaves, White Wolf and others.
