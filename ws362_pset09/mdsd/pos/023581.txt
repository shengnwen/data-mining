This is a really interesting, and enjoyably quirky, look at the role of luck in life an in business. The 'industrial strength' application of possible worlds theories can sometimes take a while to understand: why the dentist's $10m is worth more than the $10m of his lottery-winning former janitor neighbour, and so on. It's a bit all over the place, but that's half the charm. I learnt a lot, and did so smiling
