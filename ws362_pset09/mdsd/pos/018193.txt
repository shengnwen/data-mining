If you read only one book about human behavior, this should be it.  If you read many books about human behavior, this should be one of them.  Prof. Konner is wise, he is erudite, he is literate, and he is humane.  Rather than take one-sided positions or air only politically correct view, Konner synthesizes a huge amount of information and comes to sensible conclusions.
