&quot;This volume presents the proceedings of the 20th International Workshop on Graph-Theoretic Concepts in Computer Science (WG4), held in Herrsching, Germany in June 1994. The volume contains 32 thoroughly revised  papers selected from 66 submissions and provides an up-to-date snapshot of  the research performed in the field. The topics addressed are graphs  grammars, treewidth, special graph classes, algorithms on graphs,  broadcasting and architecture, planar graphs and related problems, and  special graph problems.&quot; Springe
