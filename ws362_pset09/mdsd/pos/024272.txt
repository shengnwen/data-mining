The Course in Miracles is a Cumbersome Read. The language is totally sexist, so if that matters to you, be prepared to do alot of language changes. Reading the book with others is a great help. The lessons are a creative way to break out of programmed thinking whether or not you like the book.  Slowly and gradually it begins to make sense. Forgiveness is an integral part of the context, and we all know the world could use more of that spiritual gift
