From "Political Order in Changing Societies" to "The Third Wave: Democratization in the Late Twentieth Century" to "The CLASH OF CIVILIZATIONS AND THE REMAKING OF WORLD ORDER", there is a clear logical thread from "the value of social order", "the value of democracy", to "the value of civilization". You may disagree with Huntington's multi-civilization idea, but it's all about explanatory power and it cannot be refuted until a better paradigm is suggested.
