A very gifted watercolor artist, Gordon MacKenzie's talents as a teacher really come through in his book.  He has saved the watercolor artist from many pitfalls, with his in depth guide to paint characteristics and qualities of different paints by brand.  The book contains many helpful tips on techniques, as well as &quot;how to&quot; lessons. The Watercolorist's Essential Notebook contains many examples of Mr. MacKenzie's own work
