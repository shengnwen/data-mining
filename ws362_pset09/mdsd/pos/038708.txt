Bleachers is a story of a team coming back to remember and mourn the coach they loved and hated in high school.  Football was very different back in the days and the experiences of these players is relived thru every grueling practice.  If you can quench the challenges these young men went thru you will realize what helped make them into who they are.  Grisham once again has shown is ability to change subjects.  If you are a fan of Grisham or football this is a can't miss
