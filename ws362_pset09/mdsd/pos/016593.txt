It's difficult to find an address book that isn't loaded with kitschy imagery or banal quotes:  the Typography Address Book is lovely in its simplicity, especially for those who appreciate graphic design.  Inside are found eighteen entry-spaces for most letter sub-headings, with plenty of room for two street addresses; phone, fax, and cell numbers; and e-mail addresses.  For those of us who'd like a personal reference book that doesn't look like an unwelcome present from a great-aunt, Chronicle Books has published a gem
