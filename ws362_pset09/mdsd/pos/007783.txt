I just about couldn't put this one down.  Another of Dean Koontz's more gentle novels in the vein of Odd Thomas, this was still packed with twists and turns and plenty of action.  I enjoyed the style of introducing a new idea in the first sentence of a new chapter then explaining how and why it occurred.  And the humorous exchanges between husband and wife demonstrated the intimacy that supported their life together.  It got a little long in one scene when the SUV went off the road and down the mountain, but otherwise it flows
