I love Elmore Leonard and The Hot Kid lived  up to my high expectations 
with its sparse prose, quirky characters and plenty  of action.  My 
only disappointment  is that the story takes place in Oklahoma City  
since I particularly like his novels set in Detroit.  Oh well, we 
cant always have it all.
Recently, I did read a great book set in  Detroit, Shadow of Death, by Patricia Gussin, a  taut medical suspense story where the city of Detroit shares the limelight with a lovely Detroit medical  student.
