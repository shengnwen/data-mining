This is an extremely comprehensive guide covering nearly every area of study for the NCLEX RN.  The questions and answers as well as the  rationales are all on the same page.  This illiminates the need to  continuously flip to the back of the book as in several other reviews.   Five comprehensive tests of 75 questions each are found in the back of the  book.  This allows for plenty of practice and was especially helpful on the  days immediately preceeding the exam.  It is loaded with cartoons which  give hints and words of encouragement but tend to become tedious after a  while.  This should not detract from the overall excellent review which the  over 3000 questions provide though
