The Broker by John Grisham is a great story about one man's effort to escape from his old life and start a new one. Joel Backman was pardoned from prison and hidden in Italy by the government. They were all watching to see who killed him first. They didn't count on Backman taking control of his life, though. Backman does his best to cover his tracks while trying to escape Europe to get back to America. Although slow in the beginning, this novel is a must read legal thriller
