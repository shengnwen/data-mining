The world lost a real gem of a writer when Stan Berenstain passed on November 29.  Together, Berenstain and his wife, Jan, and eventually their two sons, created books featuring lovable but flawed characters who tackle just about every real issue that faces families.  

Teaching kids about telling the truth is often a hard thing to do.  Usually, by the time the subject comes up, it's already too late - the lie has been told, figured out, punishment has been handed down, etc.  Why not use this delightful little fable to open up a dialogue about the topic before it gets out of control?
