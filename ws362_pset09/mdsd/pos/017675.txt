This book is good at understanding the strategies involved with recruiting top talent from the recruiters point of view.  If anyone is going to work with a professional executive search firm and needs to understand how they do it, I encourage you to read it this book.  This book has excellent management tips and skills! As a seasoned senior retained recruiter, I've used these strategies over and over and they have proven success. I would recommend this book to anyone and everyone, whether or not you are considering your options or not
