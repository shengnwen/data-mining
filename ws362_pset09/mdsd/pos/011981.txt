This is a fascinating book that stimulates one to observe the beauty of life's everyday processes and to record these experiences in the form of simpledrawings-- sketches that need not be exact, are often indistinct, yet  capture some intricate details to  reveal the beauty of nature. This is a  very &quot;warm and friendly&quot; book with a very rustic, imaginative  style, and is very readable. The expressions in this book have stimulated  the interest of almost all who have picked it up- my sons aged 14 and 8, my  daughter aged 4, a computer programmer, a cancer patient in the ward, a  nurse, and therapist and others from all walks of life - the artist within  all of us
