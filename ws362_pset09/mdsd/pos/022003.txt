I am a professional writer. I have had published scores of magazine features and even a couple of books.  But Bill Bryson taught me a few things and made me think about what I say and write even more than usual.  It isn't easy to admit someone is much more learned than I in these matters, but here he is.  If you have occasion to write more than an occasional email, put this book on your frequently-used reference shelf, for it is bound to become invaluable
