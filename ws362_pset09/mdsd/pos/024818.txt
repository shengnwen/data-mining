This book would be a great addition to any wildlife biologist's library.   The book covers the diseases of all the wild mammals that occur in and around Florida, from Bats to Whales.  Some of the topics covered in each  chapter are: trauma, environmental contaminants, rabies, other viruses,  bacterial diseases, mycotic infections, protozoan parasites, helminths, and  arthropods.  All the published disease literature is covered along with an  enormous amount of unpublished data contributed by researchers throughout  Florida.  Each chapter contains many tables and figures which make it easy  for the reader to find and understand the infomation presented.  Each  chapter is concluded with a summary and a detailed literature cited  section.  Anyone who deals with wildlife in Florida should have a copy of  this book
