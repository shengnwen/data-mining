Trainer thinks who and what we end up to be is simply a matter of destiny.  Sometimes it's a matter of survival.  I dog-eared and underlined tons of her sentances but I still found myself struggling with one thing: voice, and it is what every writer must ultimately find for themselves and it won't be in any book.  The courage to write your story as you see it - Trainer emphasizes that which is great and necessary - can only come from realizing we won't be here 50 years from now so say it now while you can - for yourself, others and those who come after you
