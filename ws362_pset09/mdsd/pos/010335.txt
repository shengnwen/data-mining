This book includes some of legendary photographer Irving Penn's most compelling and most complex images.  It shows his unique eye for great texture, line, space and composition in general.  He is an expert at many major areas of photography as evidenced here.  Included are examples of fine portraiture, fashion photography, photojournalism and still life.  His portraiture captures humanity through an unflinchingly real yet kindly gaze.  His fashion photography is elegantly nuanced showing the grand silhouettes, textures and cuts of vintage high fashion.  His work with the figure shows great depth, line and shape.  His incredible still lifes show a great three dimensional quality and texture as well as a grand eye for composition.  His studies of subjects in New Guinea  are brave and beautiful.  Overall a wonderful book by a cherished legend
