As a former member of Robert Roth's platoon 1056 at Parris Island, I relived the sorrows and pain experienced while in Viet Nam. Truly a book which has missed it's place along side those which are now epic movies in  their own right. Robert Roth or &quot;Abi&quot; as the Drill Instructors  called him, has inspired a book which reflects his emotion, feelings, and  intelligence. A must read for the Viet Vet searching for something, which  for most of us, is unattainable. An informative book for the younger reader  in search of understanding
