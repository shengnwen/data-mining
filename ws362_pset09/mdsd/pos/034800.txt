I'm writing to tell you that if Oprah had gotten hold of this book it would probably be on her list of the top ten books of this year. Flowers  are Forever is filled with true stories of how flowers have moved  people-from rich to poor, from novice gardener to expert. Page after page  is devoted to real life essays and stories that with out a doubt will  inspire every reader. This book has the ability to inspire even the broken  hearted. Ms. Lamancusa really showed her flair as an author when she  composed this collection. I even purchased additional copies for family and  friends to give at Christmas
