A deserved winner of several prizes, this book should be required reading for every high school senior in America.  I think it needs a fair amount of sophistication to read -- it's written by an academic, and he uses big words generously.  One thing that occasionally irritated me was a tendency to write a polemic.  These are small flaws, however, in a book that will lead all readers to appreciate the history of slavery in this country as they never have before.  Johnson reveals the psychological ground of slavery and what an all-encompassing and pernicious system it was
