The Blacker the Berry tells the story of a dark-skinned female who comes to grip withher skin color. She has to deal with racism from all around her including within her own family. She realizes that she can't change things and that she will just have to accept it and love herself for the way she is. The Blacker the Berry is a great read for everyone, not only dark-skinned people. It will teach you to love yourself and to appreciate others the way they are
