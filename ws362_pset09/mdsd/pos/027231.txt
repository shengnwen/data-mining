I led the discussion on GIFT FROM THE SEA at our book club this fall and became engrossed in the diaries of Anne Morrow Lindbergh.  What a pleasant experience to discover and read NO MORE WORDS by Reeve Lindbergh.  She writes with the same sensitivity and personal insight as her mother.  Reeve's relationship with her mother during the final days of Anne's life, reveals the frustrations and the joys of a family caregiver.  Her book is a &quot;gift&quot; to all who are or may find themselves in a similar situation
