Le morte d'Arthur (it should be La Morte d'Arthur) is a literary work that cannot be forgotten. Probably Arturus or Artorius was a roman officer trying to defend the last remains of civilization from its fall in Western Europe, but he was soon transformed into a Celtic chieftain, and much later into a medieval Norseman king. The reason is that everybody wanted to hear stories about him, and everyone wanted him to be the righteous king in times of distress. In this already old-fashioned version you will recover the legend with some XX and XIV centuries' addition to a story that had been changing and taking new shapes from V to XIV centuries but always remaining fascinating
