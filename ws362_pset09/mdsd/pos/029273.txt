Stanley's humans head out nightly allowing their dog a fast ride down the slippery slope of the good life.  Before he knows it, a little relaxing on the forbidden couch while listening to the stereo has become the canine party of the century.  Humorous, engaging, and illustrated to capture the personalities of doggies from all sides of the park, this tale is packed with chuckles and is great for read alouds to young animal-lovers everywhere.
