When our daughter was born at 25 weeks, I went out and read just about every book on preemies that I could get my hands on. This book is BY FAR the best, and every parent of a preemie should own it and read it. It is organized in any easy-to-digest manner, and is designed so that you need only read the types of things that apply to you and your baby. (For parents of multiples, there's additional great stuff, too.) This book is the one touted on the March of Dimes website--and I can see why
