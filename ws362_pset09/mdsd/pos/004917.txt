This is an intelligently written book, with a history of neurology and neuroscience, followed by an explanation of neurofeedback, and personal accounts from those who have used it. 

Readers looking for medical studies reported in medical journals can look at PubMed to find plenty. Most of them are in medically advanced foreign countries (Germany, Switzerland, the Netherlands, Korea, Russia), where modern medicine and other therapies  are not operating in different worlds the way they do in the US. Do not make the mistake of thinking that just because the US has not had that many large studies, studies have not been conducted. They have.
