If you are looking for more than &quot; Oh, Golly&quot; physics with some real mathematics, then this will be disappointing, but if you like  Richard Feynman type informative lectures that name the names and  give an understandable overview, then this is a well written book with history and facts and references. I got is as a gift from a friend who was trying to be of help! It was more help than some of my Google searches on the Internet
