Any child who watches television -- and what child doesn't? -- sees many disturbing things happening in the world.  We would all wish such things did not exist -- but they do.  The best we can do is to help children put such events in some sort of context.  Bad things happen to good people, and good people -- even our friends and neighbors -- can do horrible things.  But there are heroes and there are possibilities for understanding and for reconciliation.  "Smoky Night" teaches these lessons powerfully well
