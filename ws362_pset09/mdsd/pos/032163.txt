Ron Kovic served in Vietnam in an initially unquestioning tour of duty which ended in a terrible wounding and many new questions about the war: BORN ON THE FOURTH OF JULY provides the Vietnam vet's experiences and coming of age during the war, capturing in vivid language his evolution from a patriotic athlete to a soldier, then an anti-war activist. From ridicule and the hardening of soldiers in basic training to field fighting and disability, BORN ON THE FOURTH OF JULY provides a 'you are there' intimacy few others accomplish.
