Bernd Heinrich is  one of the most dedicated scientific observers that I have read.  Ravens have always held a fascination for me, and it is wonderful that Heinrich has taken the pains to not only study them, but to put his extensive observations and experiences into an entertaining, readable form.  As a teacher, I can refer students to his book as a record of the &quot;right&quot; way to conduct an impartial scientific test.  Ravens in Winter is destined to be the ultimate authority on the behavior of Corvus Corax, our common raven
