This book is a good general help for those interested in learning more about the bible.  It is full of colorful illustrations and charts and hits the highlights of the bible without overwhelming the reader.  The handbook  has been created by a wide scope of scholars and is therefore even handed  in its representations and information, though admittantly sometimes a bit  dry.  It provides background information on the bible, historical  information, and commentary on each section of the bible.  Overall, a solid  overview and a good reference for the beginner or intermediate student of  the bible
