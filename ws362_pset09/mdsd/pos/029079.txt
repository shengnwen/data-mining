Tacky the penguin just doesn't fit in.   He doesn't dress right, he doesn't greet right, he doesn't sing right, he doesn't dive right, and he doesn't march right.

Tacky is annoying until the hunters appear.  Hunters look for the land of sweet penguins.   They meet Tacky.

It turns out Tacky is a good guy to have around.

It's a fun little story about the odd guy who is just different.
