I am neither a parent nor a teacher, but I found this well-written, insightful book eye-opening.I bought it as a &quot;suggested reading&quot;  gift for the parents of a young girl I have been mentoring, but could not  put it down after reading the introduction! Ms. Stepp's intermingling of  real life examples with research from the experts revealed so many things  that a non-parent can do to change the world by helping children navigate  early adolescence. Her inclusion of African American children among her  subjects, and her sensitive treatment of the special problems their lives  present brought her book home for me
