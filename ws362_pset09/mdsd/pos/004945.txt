I LOVE Jodi. Also, it was about a woman struggling with motherhood, and I can relate to that, being a PPD survivor. I enjoyed the book but was also disappointed. It didn't live up to the other Jodi books I have read and loved so much.

The gist of the story is that a young girl who has an abortion and runs away from home marries a man she assumes is Prince Charming, but he isn't. He isn't bad, just not perfect. When thrust into Motherhood she buckles under the pressure and runs away. I could relate to the issues she faced as a new mom. Jodi Picoult did a wonderful job of illustrating the thoughts and feelings of a new mother.
