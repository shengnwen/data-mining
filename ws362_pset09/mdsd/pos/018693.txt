Dan Poyner's essential classic &quot;The Self-Publishing Manual&quot; was vital to me in publishing my own speech recognition books. This book, &quot;Successful Nonfiction,&quot; elegantly summarizes key factors necessary for any non-fiction writer to succeed. The best parts of the book are the passages profiling authors who have used the tips and techniques provided. The book is also elegantly designed and would make a good gift for writers, or want-to-be writers
