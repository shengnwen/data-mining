Dan Moldea dove into the RFK assasination with his eyes and mind wide open to possible alternative histories which may have lead to Sirhan Sirhan, and/or others, pulling the trigger(s). Methodically, Moldea credibly  discounts each one of the other possibilities. Then, in his 18th hour of  interviewing Sirhan, it becomes clear that the murderer is trying to con  him. Sirhan's has little else to do. Moldea certainly could have taken a  far lower tabloidesque road to help sales (it sells okay anyway). But the  fact remains that this is the seminal book on the RFK assasination,  required reading for any deep study into the Senator's death
