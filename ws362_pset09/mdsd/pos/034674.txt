Within 6 months, 4 people I knew died, including my mother. I was reading an article written by someone who also lost her mother, and in it she recommended this book.  It's a very interesting book, as all these different methods of dying are explained, what happens to the body in the process of dying.  There's a second part to this too, about how the medical profession sees death as a failure, even when keeping people alive only prolongs their agony.  I'm not "afraid" of death, more like mystified by it, and this book was a good first step in the demystification of death, so that when my time comes, I can say "OK, I'm ready.
