"Damn! Why Didn't I Write That?" is absolutely essential for the would-be writer's reference shelf. From how to honestly evaluate your idea to how to write a winning book proposal to how to effectively market your book, Mr. McCutcheon doesn't leave out anything, and his tone throughout is amicable and encouraging.
My 1st book, "5,000 Reasons to Smile...for Chicks," was published recently; I wrote the book proposal and had a contract within one month of finishing "Damn! Why Didn't I Write That?"
