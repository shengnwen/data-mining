This book has everything to make you happy. It is for ages 7 to 777! Along with Alice you fall into an Olympic size pool of great adventure where flowers talk, chess pieces come alive and everything is, "contrariwise"! You'll fall in love with all the characters in the book - Alice, Tweedeldy, Tweedeldum, Humpty-Dumpty and so on. Trust me, you'll feel lovely when you step through the looking glass!
