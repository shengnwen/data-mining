Make learning history exciting by reading this book along with The Politically Incorrect Guide to American History by Thomas E. Woods Jr PHD.  The Sociologist Loewen provides an engaging interpretation of history with a heaping helping of left wing ideology. The Historian Woods provides a captivating analysis of history just as interesting as Loewen with a more conservative slant. When you read these books be prepared to find out some disturbing facts that lurk in our country's past.
