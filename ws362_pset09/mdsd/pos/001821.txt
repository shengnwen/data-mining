   I immigrated to Canada over 30 years ago. Having studied in a Chinese college, and been taught English grammar meticulously, I missed seeing that in the curriculum of the elementary and high school systems of my children. When I discovered Diana Hacker's manual among the books of my youngest son while he was studying for his second degree I breathed a sigh of relief and was delighted. 

   Diana covers a great deal of relevant topics in 227 pages (4th edition) and writes in a simple style easy to understand. It takes a master to write like this. When you can see through a clear blue sea it does not seem too deep. I will check into the other books of Diana, but this one I will recommend to all my medical residents. English is something that they have taken for granted but most of them cannot write good English. This is a friendly non-intimidating book that they will be receptive to
