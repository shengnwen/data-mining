Wicked Ties is a GREAT book.  Fantastic Cover!! You will not be able to lay this book down until you are finished...except maybe to take a cold shower!!! This book is a scorcher! HOT HOT HOT!!

Shayla writes a great story with a hook that will keep you interested.  Jack and Morgan have an instant vibe and the twist with Deke makes it all the more interesting.  This book will leave you hot, bothered, and anxiously waiting for more from Shayla.  

Can't wait for the next book with Deke
