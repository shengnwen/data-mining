This work helps to acquaint one with the life and experiences of a southern family who live in Georgia during the Civil War era.  The Jones family are tightly knitt clan, who support their beloved confederate state  until the end.  No matter what happens in their life, they believe in the  end that the LORD will help them out and show them the way. A must for  anyone with an interest in the Civil War. You come to understand the  southern planter experience in this unique piece
