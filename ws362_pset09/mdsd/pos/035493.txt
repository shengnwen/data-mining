Whether you agree or disagree w/ Neuman's models of citizenship, his place in immigration law and constitutional theory is solid.  He reconstructs the history of immigration, and conducts his questions of citizenship that is  bound to pave new ways for thinking about the alien/non-alien split.  This  is one of my three top books on constitutional theory along w/ Haney-Lopez,  White By Law, and Hirsch's A Theory of Liberty.  A Classic
