Jim the lover of aviation, lives in Shanghai with both his parents. As WWII unravels, the Japanese invade Shanghai. All Europeans are collected and sent to prinson camps.
Jim is seperated from his parents in Shanghai, then he is caught...........
He joins up with Baisy in one of the camps, the relationship between Jim and Baisy is a strange and not quiet understandable, it's a survival of the fittest situation.
Jim grows up and learns to except the war, he begins to like it.
I don't want to spoil the rest, it's a very powerful story; one worth reading.
