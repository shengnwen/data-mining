With the identity of Deep Throat now known, All the President's Men has an added bit of excitement.  I couldn't help but think, "wasn't it obvious?" as I read it and thought of Deep Throat's true identity.  Reading it, I realized that Woodward and Bernstein didn't try to hide the secret as well as all the speculation over the last 30 years would lead one to believe.
