The oral histories will pull at your heart strings  and make you look for ways to help the Mayans in Guatemala.  The accounts have such vivid nighmare  quality images, you will find it hard to put down. The introduction by Noam Chomsky is a little dry, but very helpful in letting the reader know what is really going on with the CIA and Guatemala.  If you like this book, you should also look at &quot;Searching  for Everado&quot;, Harburry's second book about her search  for her husband after he is captured by the Guatemalan  military
