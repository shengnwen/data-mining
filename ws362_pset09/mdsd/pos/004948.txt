Lorenzo Smythe has to be one of the best examples of a character you love to loathe in the beginning, and winding up loving by the middle. The way that Smythe, like so many of Heinlen's first person narrators, reveals things about himself, sometimes inadvertently, is just as interesting as the stories he tells. &quot;Double Star&quot;, as far as I'm concerned, is his Heinlen's virtuoso performance of narration, and a terrific plot to go with it
