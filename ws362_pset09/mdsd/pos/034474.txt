Once again, the Firstime Prophet Texe Marrs, Son of Lucifer himself, paints a dire picture of the future of mankind. There will be no salvation for this forsaken race.  We are doomed. Marshall Applewhite and the Heaven's Gate suicides presaged the forthcoming destruction of all Humankind, enslaved to Satan forevermore. However, not even those like Applewhite will escape the grip of Lucifer, who has defiled Heaven, Hell and Earth alike
