In the late 40's and early 50's, my sisters and I were subjected to our mother's "good cooking," the ilk of which is accurately represented in this volume. My sisters and I screamed with uncontrollable mirth/disgust as we realized that our problems with eating and food are explained, in part, by the kinds of 'recipes' described in this book. Like black humor - someone sticks a knife in your ribs until it tickles
