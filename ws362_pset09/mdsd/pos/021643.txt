Chunky Knits by knitting expert Carolyn Clewer presents the &quot;do-it-yourself&quot; needlecraft enthusiast with fourteen quick and simple knitting projects that anyone can use to create wool sweaters, pull-overs and turtlenecks. Featuring thick rolls of yarn and a flair for creativity as well as practicality, Chunky Knits is enhanced throughout with full color photography and extremely detailed, step-by-step instructions ideal for knitters of all skill and experience levels. Chunky Knits an excellent and highly commended resource and reference for anyone looking to create their own homemade, hand-crafted, practical, and esthetically impressive needlecraft treasures
