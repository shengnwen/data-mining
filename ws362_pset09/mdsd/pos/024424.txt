An enlightening and dispiriting account of the rise and fall of President Clinton's health care reform plan. Broder and Johnson interviewed all the principals, from President Clinton and Hillary Clinton down to uninsured individuals dealing with serious health problems. Diagnosis: Change the U.S. health care system to provide all Americans with access to basic health care. Prescription: Massive federal program to expand the health insurance market and control costs.
