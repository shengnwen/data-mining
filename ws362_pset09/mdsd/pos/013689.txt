Lachapelle is a master of digital manipulation and, in fact, would probably concur that he is more a &quot;conceptual artist&quot; [employing digital technology as a template]than a fine art photographer, wherein what he sees through the lens is what is presented. His work dazzles because the &quot;reality&quot; in it isn't any more real that the &quot;Starship Enterprise.&quot; This doesn't diminish the dazzling effect the images have on the viewer. Stitching together all those pesky pixels took a lot of time and for that alone I purchased his book
