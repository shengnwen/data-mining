This memoir is absolutely rewarding.  It starts out in Chicago where Sidney is born and raised by his hardworking parents. The novel covers his adventurous trail to New York City where he is employed in low wage jobs and then later to California.  With luck and hard work, he rises to the top as a writer, producer and director.  His many books have reached millions and are read world-wide  Many have been made into movies. This 80+ gentleman has done the world a favor with his writing ability
