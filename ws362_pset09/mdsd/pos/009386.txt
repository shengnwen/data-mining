Wisdom and humor are typical of Curran, but she really outdoes herself this time in &quot;Tired of Arguing&quot;.  This book  gives practical suggestions to parents around issues from chronic whining to monster spray.  I underlined most of it and laughed out loud over and over again at her  ingenious responses to children's tedius arguments.     Curran gives  practical help to parents who fall into the &quot;anger trap&quot; and  shows how humor is both  a great defense and offense in the game of  parenting. The &quot;Handy Responses to Kids' Arguments&quot; was my  favorite part of the book...straightforward, honest,and effective! Anyone  with a child in the argumentative years (ie, age 3-23) should have this  book
