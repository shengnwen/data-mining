Driven is the story of a young man who is unfortunately the product of his environment.  The saying, "A son pays for his father's sins" is an accurate depiction.  

What can you do when your father dies and your mother goes to jail?
You try and survive. That's Nasir's plan, but with so many people bringing him drama it becomes impossible to do.       
 
Driven is a well told story leaving the reader anxious for the rest. 

Reviewed by: Angie
