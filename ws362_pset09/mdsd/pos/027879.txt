Through a series of thoughtful exercises, this book helps you take inventory. What are your interests, talents, experiences, education, style, priorities, gifts, and passions? How can you combine them to create a career that reflects you better than any job you've ever had? This book guides you to the answer. This is not a book that gives you a test and tells you that, based on the results, you should be a plumber. Instead it shows you how to create your own toolbox of skills and interests, offers plenty of suggestions and examples, and encourages you to find the right path to your ideal career. This book guided me through a successful career change, and I have recommended it to several friends considering the same
