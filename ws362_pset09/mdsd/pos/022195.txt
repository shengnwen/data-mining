Great read.  A fascinating start to a terrific series.  Elena is the only female werewolf and she left her pack to pursue her own life, but when troubles occur she feels inclined to return back to her old home.  With rogue werewolves and killer werewolves Elena is in for a hell of time, but she has fun with her job, but then the attention gets turned to her and her current boyfriend and her ex hangs around her alot.  With love conflicts between the two men and the fact that she is a target for one seriously bad werewolf, Elena will never seem to lead a normal life
