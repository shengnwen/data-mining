Beautifully written in the author's poetic voice, Secrets is a groundbreaking book for women on the joys and rewards of participating in a community of writers. Author Rosemary Daniell encourages women to explore their own "feminine zone" by writing with honesty about their deepest longings and most heart-breaking experiences. A multi-faceted gem, Secrets not only provides a tantalizing glimpse into the writing workshops that Daniell has led for many years, but also offers practical advice on how to move a writing career forward. If you are a writer-or have ever dreamed of writing-this book belongs on your shelf.
