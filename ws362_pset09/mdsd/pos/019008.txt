   Some writers mange to transend their genre and Jim Steinmeyer is one such writer. Even if you our not a fan of magic you cannot help but but enjoy steinmeyer's glorious journey through the magics golden era. Chung Ling Soo is hard subject to nail down. For his real life deceptions were as numerous as his stage illusions.But Steinmeyer through extensive research has brought his diffcult character to life.In a sense the book is not just about Ching Ling Soo because magic is a small closed world and they interact mostly with each other. So their are many delightful cameo portraits of other famous magicians such as Houdini and Keller.With his other work " Hiding the Elephant " Steinmeyer has established himself as the finist writer of magic history today
