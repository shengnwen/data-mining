Simply a wonderful book.  Starting before her first book &quot;We the Living&quot;, continuing through her masterpiece &quot;Atlas Shrugged&quot;, to the final years of her life, this is Ayn Rand's  development as a writer and a thinker--as only she could show it. You will  see her accept commonly held bad ideas early in her career, only to later  discover their flaws and repudiate them.  If you are interested in your own  development as a thinker, there is no better guide than this account of the  development of a master
