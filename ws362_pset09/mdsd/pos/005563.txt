Filled cover to cover with instructional color photographs, Basic Scenery For Model Railroaders: The Complete Photo Guide by Lou Sassi is a finely detailed, step-by-step how-to guide for creating picturesque, professional-looking, three-dimensional, model railroading scenery. Explicit and meticulous instructions unfailingly guide novice and expert hobbyists alike in creating woodlands, ponds, roads, rural landscapes, and more to perfect scale. Basic Scenery For Model Railroaders is very highly recommended for serious model railroad hobbyists
